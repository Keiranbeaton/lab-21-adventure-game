Buckaroo Banzai Across the 8th Dimension - The Game

Buckaroo Banzai and his men, the Hong Kong Cavliers, have been placed in a building with no exits. In order to stop the evil plans of Dr. Emilio Lizardo, they must rescue the four black lectroids before they are caught by Dr. Lizardo's red lectroids. Move from room to room to find black lectroids. Good luck, the fate of the world is in your hands.
